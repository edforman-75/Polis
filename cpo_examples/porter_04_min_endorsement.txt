September 2, 2025

REP. DAVE MIN ENDORSES KATIE PORTER TO BE CALIFORNIA'S NEXT GOVERNOR

Rep. Dave Min (CA-47) announced his endorsement of Katie Porter for California Governor.

"Katie is a true fighter for the people... She's not afraid to stand up for what's right and deliver for Californians," said Rep. Dave Min.

"I'm so grateful to have Rep. Min's support in this race... Dave knows a thing or two about winning tough elections," said Katie Porter.

The endorsement highlights Porter's campaign momentum, including leading recent polls and receiving endorsements from organizations like UAW.

Min praised Porter's focus on addressing affordability issues like child care, housing, and healthcare costs.

Porter emphasized her commitment to standing up against Trump as he "lobb[ies] attack after attack at our state."
